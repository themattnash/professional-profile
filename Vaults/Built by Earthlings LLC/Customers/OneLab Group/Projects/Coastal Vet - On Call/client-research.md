## Business Overview

Coastal Vet is a **women-owned, membership-based concierge veterinary clinic** serving cats and dogs in Virginia Beach, VA. Founded in 2022 by Dr. Leigh Hofmeister and Dr. Nicole Cromwell, the practice is modeled after the MDVIP concierge medicine concept applied to veterinary care — intentionally limiting patient load to deliver a premium, highly personalized experience.

The core differentiator is *quality over quantity*: each veterinarian caps enrollment at **50 households**, ensuring long-term, relationship-driven care rather than the high-volume, transactional model common in traditional vet practices.

## Business Model: Membership-Only Concierge Care

Coastal Vet does **not** accept non-member patients. All care is delivered through a tiered monthly membership structure. New clients start with a free health consultation before committing.

### Membership Tiers

| Tier | Price | Key Inclusions |
|---|---|---|
| **Access** | Starting ~$35/mo | 24/7 licensed access, same/next-day sick visits, after-hours emergency, telehealth ($50/session, applied to in-person if needed), health & nutrition consult |
| **Premier** | $90–$145/mo | All Access + unlimited 24/7 telehealth, annual comprehensive screening, mid-year recheck, treatment exam, all vaccinations, bloodwork, heartworm/tick/fecal screening, flea/tick/HW prevention |
| **Premier Plus** | Starting ~$225/mo | All Premier + unlimited exams, at-home concierge service, managed specialist care, same-day home drop-off/pickup, extended hours, overnight surgical boarding |

- **Enrollment fee:** $50
- **Add-ons available:** dental, nutrition, supplements, therapy, surgery
- **Insurance:** Accepts Trupanion; works with other providers

## Brand & Positioning

**Tagline/Positioning:** Higher quality, personalized care for cats and dogs.

**Target customer:** Pet owners who treat their animals as family members and are willing to pay a premium for a relationship-based veterinary experience with direct vet access, no rushed appointments, and true 24/7 availability.

**Tone:** Warm, premium, trust-forward. The brand emphasizes personal relationships, proactive health (not just illness management), and convenience.

**Differentiators vs. traditional vet practices:**
- Direct phone/text/email access to your vet (not a call center)
- Same-day appointments guaranteed
- Capped enrollment for consistent attention
- Transparent pricing (no surprise costs)
- Separate dog and cat facilities

## Key Observations for Project Context

1. **On-call / after-hours is central to their brand.** 24/7 access to a licensed professional is a core membership benefit and a primary selling point. Any tooling built around "on-call" workflows should reinforce — not undercut — this promise.

2. **Small, high-trust operation.** With only 2 veterinarians and hard enrollment caps (50 households/vet), the team is small and relationships are tight. Solutions should be lightweight, not add operational overhead.

3. **Premium client expectations.** Members are paying $35–$225+/mo and expect immediate, personal responses. Any on-call system needs to feel human-grade — delays or impersonal responses would damage the brand.

4. **Women-owned, local, award-winning.** This is a community-embedded brand with strong local recognition. Clients chose them deliberately; they are not price-sensitive but are quality-sensitive.

5. **Membership advisor (Lisa Hoberg) is a key operational role.** May be a stakeholder for any workflow or communication tooling.
